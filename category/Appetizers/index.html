Bread Rolls

Ingredients

½ Chicken soupstock
6 bread slices
Salt
1 tbsp soya sauce
1 tsp garlic
¼ tsp black peper,
½ tsp red chillies crushed
½ cup knorr tomato ketchup
¼ cup boiled corn
¼ cup spring onion
¼ cup capsicum
1 beaten egg
Bread crumbs for coating
2 tbsp oil
½ Chicken soupstock
6 bread slices
Salt
1 tbsp soya sauce
1 tsp garlic
¼ tsp black peper,
½ tsp red chillies crushed
½ cup knorr tomato ketchup
¼ cup boiled corn
¼ cup spring onion
¼ cup capsicum
1 beaten egg
Bread crumbs for coating
2 tbsp oil

Directions

Heat oil Fry garlic, then chicken soupstock. Add all the seasoning and then ketchup. Put vegetables and cook for a while. Take bread slice in hand, soak it in water and squeeze the excess water. Put chicken filling in the center and make a ball shape Dip in beaten egg and coat with bread crumbs. Deep fry till golden brown. Serve hot



Chana Daal Samosa

Ingredients

Boiled Gram lentils (Chana Dal) 1 cup
Onion (finely chopped) 1 medium onion
Green chili (chopped) 1
Cumin 1 tbsp
Roasted coriander seeds 1 tbsp
Red chili (crushed) 2 tsp
Ginger (sliced) 1sp
Oil 1 tbsp
Knorr chicken soupstock 10gm
Fresh coriander leaves 1/2 cup
Samosa pastry (Manda/Samosa patti)

Directions

Heat oil and fry cumin and coriander. Add onion and ginger one by one. Mix and set to cool. Take mixture and add to samosa patti. Make triangles. Fry till golden. Serve with salsa.



Chicken Croquettes

Ingredients

1 Cup Potatoes
1 Cup Chicken Mince
½ Cup Fresh Bread Crumbs
½ Tsp Garlic,
Salt To Taste
½ Tsp Black Peper
½ Tsp White Peper
Filling: ¼ Cup Cheedar Cheese
1 Tsp Green Chillies (Chopped)
2 Tbsp Knorr Tomato Ketchup
Other Ingredients: 2 Beaten Eggs & Bread Crumbs

Directions

Mix Boiled Mashed Potatoes With Chicken Mince And Bread Crumbs With All The Seasonings. Take A Small Quantity Of The Mixture And Put The Filling In The Center. Make Croquette Shaped Balls. Dip In Beaten Egg and Coat with Bread Crumbs. Deep Fry till Golden Brown. Serve Hot.



Chicken Sausage Rolls

Ingredients

4 Chicken sausages
5gm Mustard sauce
10 ml Knorr Tomato Ketchup
1 sliced Onion
2 Lettuce leaves
2 Parathas, Salt to taste
3 gm Crushed pepper corn
10 gm Margarine

Directions

Take the chicken sausages and grill on tawa. Take the onion and peel it. Make onion slices and keep it aside. Take hot paratha & spread Mustard sauce on top. Place lettuce, the chicken sausages and onion on paratha. Pour Knorr tomato ketchup. Serve hot.
