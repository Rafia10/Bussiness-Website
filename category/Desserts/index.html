Kulfa Ice Cream

Ingredients

Whipped cream 1 cup
Evaporated milk ½ tin
Condensed milk ½ tin
Almonds and pistachios ½ cup(grinned)
Small green cardamoms 4-5
A pinch of saffron in milk.
Fresh coconut ½ cup (desiccated)
Bread 2 slices

Directions

Mix all the ingredients in a blender and blend until well combined. Pour in a plastic container and freeze until firm.



Labb e Shereen

Ingredients

1 liter Milk
2 Banana
150g Mango
100g Red Jelly
½ cup Custard Powder
100g Vermicelli
½ cup Sugar
1 tbsp Vanilla Essence
50g Raisin
50g Almond Chopped
50g Pistachio Chopped
½ cup Cream
150g Fruits

Directions

In a pan, add milk and heat it for a while. Now, add custard powder mixed with little milk. Now, add vermicelli and cook for 5 minutes. Now, add Sugar, vanilla essence, raisins, almonds, pistachio and cook for 2-3 minutes then cool it. Now, add mix fruits, mango, banana, grapes, cream, red jelly, and mix it well. Your tasty Lab-e-Shireen now ready to serve



Special Falooda

Ingredients

4 cup Full cream milk
1 cup condensed milk
1 tbsp Pistachio (crushed)
1 tbsp Almond (crushed)
1 tbsp Corn flour
1 pinch Saffron (optional)
1 tsp Cardamom powder
Ingredients for vermicelli: 1/2 cup Corn flour
1 & 1/2 cup Water
1 tbsp Sugar
1/2 tsp Basil seeds
1 cup Water
Ingredients for garnish: Red syrup as required
Almonds crushed as required
Pistachio crushed as required
Jelly as required

Directions

In a pan boil 3 & 1/2 cup of milk and add saffron. As soon as it starts to leave color add cardamom powder, pistachio and almond in it. Cook on medium heat until the milk left 3 cups. Add condensed milk and cook for 2 to 3 minutes. Mix 1/2 cup of milk with 1 tablespoon of corn flour. Add this mixture in milk and cook for 10 minutes. Turn off the flame, bring rabri to room temperature and refrigerate for 2-3 hours before use. In a pan mix water, corn flour and sugar well. Cook it on medium to low flame. Keep stirring until the mixture becomes transparent. Take nimko maker fill it with vermicelli mixture and make vermicelli in chilled water. Keep them in water and take out when you are about to serve. Note: the vermicelli mixture should be hot while making or else it will become hard. In one cup of water soak basil seeds for about 10 to 15 minutes.



Chocholate Cake

Ingredients

Plain Flour 2 cups
Cream 2 cups
Icing Sugar ½ cup
Chocolate Sauce ½ cup
Butter 1 cup

Chocolate bar 1 pack Sugar 2 cups
Eggs 6
Coco Powder 4tbsp
Gelatin powder 3 tbsp

Directions

In a a double boiler melt a chocolate bar. For Sponge: In a mixing bowl take 6 eggs and beat it until it forms foam. Add baking powder, sugar, and butter and beat. Add half cup chocolate sauce and mix. Add flour and fold it, when all the ingredients are dissolved in each other then grease a cake pan and put the batter in it. Place it in an oven at 160 degrees, bake for 20 minutes. When the cake sponge is done turn it upside down on a wire rack. Allow to cool and cut it from the centre. Make a mixture of cream, cocoa powder, and icing sugar, spread it on one half of the sponge and place the other half on it. In a bowl take water and sugar and make syrup. Spread it on the cake. Now add the remaining chocolate in chocolate sauce and dissolve gelatin in half boiled water and mix well. Spread this mixture on the cake, place it in a refrigerator for ½ hour to chill and then serve
