Biryani & Pulao


Biryani & Pulao

Biryani is love for pakistani people. They eat different varieties of biryani such as Sindhi biryani, afghani biryani,punjabi biryani etc. Biryani is the king of South Asians cuisines. And We are here to make you learn that how you can cook biryani by youselves at home easily.




Sindhi Biryani

Ingredients

Chicken 1 kg
Rice 2 ½ cup
Plum 5 - 6
Red chilli powder 1 ½ tbsp
Turmeric powder 1 tsp
Garam masala 1 tsp
Coriander powder 2 tbsp
Ginger garlic paste 1 ½ tbsp
Tomato 4
Potato 4
Green chilli 6
Mint 1 bunch
Lemon 4
Yogurt 1 ½ cup
Onion 4 (sliced)
Milk 1 cup
Food colour 1 pinch
Oil 1 ½ cup

Directions

Put oil in a pan and pre heat oil and cook onion in it till it become golden brown. Then take it out on absorbent sheet. Now put chicken, salt and ginger garlic paste and cook it till water dries. Blend brown onion, turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala, chili powder and yogurt together Add the mixture in chicken along with plum and stir fry. Cook it for 5 minutes and add ½ cup of water. Boil rice by adding salt, little mint leaves and green chili, drain when its partially cooked. Fry tomato and potato, put lemon juice, mint and green chilli in chicken and mix it well. Layer half of rice and spread chicken batter on top of it, then put remaining rice. Now add mint and green chilli and add food color dissolved in milk, pour on top, cover the lid and simmer. When steam comes out dish it out.



Afghani Pulao

Ingredients

Chicken ½ kg (boiled)
Rice 250 gm
Salt ½ tsp
Oil ¼ cup
Garlic 1 tsp
Ginger 1 tsp
Onion 1 cup
Almond ¼ cup (crushed)
Yogurt ¼ cup
Butter 2 tb

Directions

Heat oil and fry onion and take out half onion. Then fry boiled chicken, ginger, garlic with yogurt for five minutes. Now boil rice and fry almond in butter. Now put rice and chicken in pan/wok and steam for two minutes. Then take out in dish and sprinkle onion and almond. Delicious Afghani Pulao is ready to serve.



Chicken Pulao

Ingredients

Chicken 1 kg
Oil ½ cup
Onions 2 (large, sliced)
Yogurt ¼ cup
Rice 1 kg (soaked)
Almonds few (sliced and fried)
Bay leaves 2
Cinnamon 1" piece
Cloves 5
Cardamom 2 - 3
Black peppercorn 10 - 12
Ginger paste 1 tbsp
Garlic paste 1 tbsp
Red chili powder 1 tsp
Coriander seeds powder 2 tsp
Cumin seed powder 1 tsp
Turmeric ¼ tsp
Saffron ½ tsp
Salt to taste

Directions

Heat the oil and fry onions till golden brown. Add whole spices fry for few minutes then add powdered spices taking care not to burn. Now add ginger garlic paste along with chicken. Put beaten yogurt and fry chicken till water dries. Add soaked rice with 2 cups of hot water and salt to taste. Cook on low heat with the lid on until the rice is cooked. Dissolve saffron in 2 tbsp water and pour over the pulao. To serve, place the rice in a large serving dish or platter. Place the chicken pieces on top. Garnish fried almonds and serve with raita.
